A.I. Wellness Companion

A non-clinical, supportive check-in tool for reflection and gentle guidance.

About this Prototype

This is a school project prototype of a mental wellness assistant. It is designed for reflection and self-care ideas, not for diagnosis or treatment.

Important Disclaimer

This assistant is not a therapist, does not give medical advice, and should not be used for emergencies. It cannot see, hear, or monitor you. It only responds to what you type.

Crisis & Safety

If you are in crisis, thinking about harming yourself or someone else, or feel unsafe:

  • Call or text 988 in the U.S. for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
  • Call your local emergency number (such as 911).
  • Reach out to a trusted friend, family member, or professional.

This prototype will try to detect some crisis language and redirect you to real-world support, but it is not a replacement for human help.

Suggested Use

You can use this assistant to:

  • Check in with your mood
  • Reflect on your day or week
  • Get gentle coping ideas and grounding exercises
  • Clarify what you might want to talk about with a real person
